Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by grade level. Generally, we require previous academic records, an entrance assessment, and an interview with both the student and parents.

What is the school's curriculum?

Benhill English School follows the CBSE curriculum with additional emphasis on language skills, mathematics, sciences, and holistic development through co-curricular activities.

Are there transportation services available?

Yes, we provide transportation services covering major routes around Iritty. The exact routes and schedules are available at the administrative office.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports, music, dance, art, debate, robotics, and various clubs to foster all-round development of our students.
