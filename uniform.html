Kindergarten

Boys Daily Uniform

Our kindergarten boys wear comfortable white short-sleeved shirts paired with maroon shorts. This practical uniform allows for ease of movement during play and learning activities.

Optional items: Maroon cardigan for cooler days

Designs may slightly vary

Girls Daily Uniform

Our kindergarten girls wear a white short-sleeved blouse with a maroon pinafore dress. This charming uniform is both practical and comfortable for active young learners.

Optional items: Maroon cardigan, white hair accessories

Designs may slightly vary

Primary School

Boys Daily Uniform

Primary school boys wear a crisp white long-sleeved shirt with a maroon tie and dark blue trousers. This smart uniform helps instill a sense of pride and belonging.

Optional items: Dark blue V-neck sweater, school blazer for formal occasions

Designs may slightly vary

Girls Daily Uniform

Primary school girls wear a white long-sleeved blouse with a maroon tie and dark blue pleated skirt. This elegant uniform promotes a sense of community and school identity.

Optional items: Dark blue cardigan, school blazer for formal occasions

Designs may slightly vary

Middle School

Boys Daily Uniform

Middle school boys wear a white long-sleeved shirt with a striped maroon and dark blue tie, paired with dark blue trousers. This refined uniform reflects their growing maturity.

Optional items: Dark blue blazer with school crest, V-neck sweater for cooler weather

Designs may slightly vary

Girls Daily Uniform

Middle school girls wear a white long-sleeved blouse with a striped maroon and dark blue tie, complemented by a dark blue knee-length pleated skirt. This smart uniform fosters school pride.

Optional items: Dark blue blazer with school crest, cardigan for cooler weather

Designs may slightly vary

Senior School

Boys Daily Uniform

Senior school boys wear a formal white long-sleeved shirt with a dark blue and maroon striped tie, dark blue blazer, and trousers. This distinguished uniform prepares them for professional settings.

Optional items: Dark blue V-neck sweater, prefect badge for student leaders

Designs may slightly vary

Girls Daily Uniform

Senior school girls wear an elegant white long-sleeved blouse with a dark blue and maroon striped tie, dark blue blazer, and knee-length pleated skirt. This polished uniform instills confidence and professionalism.

Optional items: Dark blue cardigan, prefect badge for student leaders

Designs may slightly vary
